Hi friend! I’m Leo, a student studying Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin.

My interest in engineering began with my interest in magic. I loved being able to learn and create my own tricks. However, my parents rarely purchased magic tricks for me online so I had to watch demo videos, figure the tricks out, and then handcraft my own gimmicks. I enjoyed the process of approaching a project with no idea on how to do it and then figuring out a way to make something useful with my hands. Subsequently, I thought that software engineering might be an appropriate outlet for creating something "magical."

Outside of my engineering life, I’m a sneaker head, food junkie, amateur rapper, tennis player, Apple fanboy, and Green Bay Packer fan!
